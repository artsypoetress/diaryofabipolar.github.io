Misundestood.

Our love misunderstood . How do you expect a broken soul to love you, when all they’ve ever been had been an emotional havoc, Unhealed wounds that almost always bled on those who inflicted no harm to me, and here I am counting stars, since the moon that ever made into my life never stayed long enough.

Even though No one has ever left me, I have left countless I’ve truly loved. scared of abandonment to a point, where I don’t even know how to commit anymore and so I leave before anyone and everyone else.

I rather be the villain, than suffer being left, nobody understands me; I try to make evens trust me I try, but all they see is odds. I love so hard, and I love in silence; I always fail to express what I feel, and so my love never feels real. Even when it’s the only truth in their lives.

I’ve died a million deaths, I’ll die a million more..

And this heart has already experienced a thousand years of doom, a thousand years of stars colliding with my soul’s horizon, every time it breaks a little more, and I feel the light within me trying to break out through the crevices, endlessly in search for , a new home It wanders, It hopes, to live THROUGH this reincarnated form that has probably experienced a bazillion years of chaos I wonder.

a million years of broke, I’ve died a million deaths, I’ll die a million more..

as my mind consoles my breath, but what it doesn’t know is that my breath is racing to the point where I’m inevitably on the verge OF passing out again, do you hear me screaming through that amplified silence, that makes no soul in doubt of the momentum of pain that I hold?

if you could see my heart, you would cry a million tears, and if you see it now, you would cry a million more my undead love, forcefully buried inside of me along with the other baggage of agony that I hold.

While not having you, haunts me perpetually, what’s more beautiful that my unrequited love for you? My love ocean deep, Which I fail to express, and you fail to read. Only now it doesn’t exist anymore,

I’m quick to move on, quicker to heal, Life have given me so much pain, that this stands equivalent to nothing in comparison to it.

To love, and to be loved, oh what a beautiful catastrophe. to understand and to be understood, Am I dreaming? Or asking for too much? Or maybe I’m just searching for my answers in all the wrong people, in all the wrong places.
