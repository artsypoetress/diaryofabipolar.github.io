warmth and chills.

she cries in embers of flames, charring her happiness, until all that remains is a ripped apart, bruised, wretched dead heart, how do you fail to hear her silent screams? when All she needed was genuinety.

all life all I've ever seen is deception, while all I've ever been searching for has been the truth, where do we find the sincerity, when all we see are blatant lies.. she lives on the fumes of her resilience, on the verge of passing out, until it completely consumes of her, until she's not even the same anymore.. until Then what’s then ever left within her, strength, through which nothing can seep through. her walls higher than your will to ever break it.. dangerous. if she's ever gone, who would know? truth? no one.. that's how she yearns to live and to die? remember?

what ever remains within are ashes of her soul, so acquainted to burning, (a part of her has turnt to ashes,(dead) and that being her soul). you look at her..and all you see is life, but you fail to see that she's died.. its not her, she's long lost..

for burning is all she knows, she burnt for them, and now she burns for someone else, yet she's been the source of hope for a countless, when all she experiences if reckoning, burns out to give life, burns out to be the light, fueled by the hopes rooted deep into her,

oh how, beautiful it is to dwell into the scorching heart that has now become your warmth (people look upto her for warmth, but little do they know its just scorching heat she’s suffocating on, and she knows how to give just a part of it, so people don’t burn/burn out, quite like her)

if only they knew, she’s either heartless or completely heartful, no in between, decieves some, baffles most..dont take it personally she has absolutely no control over these forms that try to overpower her, yet she tries, she fights, she conceals pain and talks in love,

for when she burns, she lights up the room and when she’s quite, she leaves you freezing in the coldest part of nights, all by yourself.

She’s only been burnt too much or been completely burnt out. She’s always had fire in her eyes, intoxicates some, intimidates the others, scares some, invite some, burns some, wrecks others, yet if you ever dare to read it, you see a poetry, a landscape compressed into the windows of her soul, as she speaks, you're only feeling chills down your spine, as she writes, your completley frozen, numb, who see a part of you in her, you see you, you see halos and horns, you see darkness thats overpowered by the light, you see wisdom and regrets, you see pain and love, you see hope and resilience, you see everything you are or yearn to be, you see chills and warmth, you see fire and ice, on the battlefield, of life, you see your yin in yan,

you sigh, but what inspires you to write they ask? pain and oblivion hold my hand, while my heartbeat like handwriting flows, through these pages, I burn as I live on the embers of these flames.
