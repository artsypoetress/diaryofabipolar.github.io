Living on two ends of the heartbeat.

Stuck in this dejavu, as I’m trying to hold taut onto a string, that’s slowly pulling me apart; Breaking me a little more, as I go from left to right, to left to right.. One pulling me towards anxiety, the other towards highs and eternal doom

I SCREAM: SET ME FREE. while I fail to release myself from these chains that have chosen me out of everyone else , while I'm screaming internally, loud, loud for everything within me to break apart, but I dont make a sound, its very much unethical to scream out loud you know. I wish I had the liberty. but, oh well! fate. if anyone would hear me scream, I'd swear to you they'd take me to a mental asylum, and so my emotions are buried until it consumes my heart.

chained on that rope, that might break me, before breaking itself apart I’ve been holding myself up for long enough, how long more? Tell me how long more?

Constrained, numb yet we try to fight our way out, we need closure more than everything else

Trying to reclaim the peace that we once lost very long ago, but all were ever left with, is living on by holding onto this tug of war

it tries to overpower us, we pray everyday to set ourselves free from this constant battle between the deceptive head and the delusional heart, these gray lights and pitch darkness,

But trust me when I say, we only really need help, when our highs and our lows merge, when there is this insignificant amount of time between them both, that it makes us question ourselves on some days? Is this really us? Or are we like possesed?

when it collides, we need to be truly saved, we badly do, we need someone to call our own or just hold us tight and not let go, that’s when we ever need love, but all we ever get and will is ourselves.

living on two ends of this heartbeat, dazed and confused, while the entire world romanticizes sad like it’s the new black, taking drugs to reach to our highs, and crashing to our lows soon after, well atleast they create their own reckoning, were born with it.

we try so hard to get a grip onto our emotions, but the tighter we grip the faster it slips, Quite so much like when we try to grip snow but it only ends up leaving us feeling so much more numb than we felt trying to hold it

We push people away, yes, how do you expect us, to hold onto something considering were stone cold, numb, yearning for warmth, consolation and love. We push and we push far away, sometimes we push away those we truly loved, forever. when In reality all we ever wanted was them to break our barriers, hug us tight and not let go.

Stranded, trying to figure out whom we really are, are we the highs, the lows or the in betweens that somehow never seems to last Before you know it, Mania kicks in Being able to feel mania must feel like bliss right? AHAHAHA YES But you do realize it all comes with a price to pay.

half the time we cant even control the damaging things we end up doing, in this stage were more like; oops I didn’t mean to through that phase of life and we leave that phase with lifelong scars on everything that’s supposedly important to us and somehow these seasons never tend to cease you know

And before you know it, winter is coming...lingering into our lives, uninvited without no second warnings We try so hard to constantly battle these emotions without letting anyone know anything of it, every single day.

The raging storms in our minds had no escape. We constantly buried it deep inside our souls, Until it reached our threshold. And we couldn’t bear anymore

somewhere in between these roller-coaster of emotions and trying to keep our shit together, we realized we have absolutely no one to blindly trust, partially because no one has ever given us a reason to.

us being paranoid is what brings us here to write anyways Because we know for a certain fact a friend has the power to deceive you These inks do not..
