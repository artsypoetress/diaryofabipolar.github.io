What’s the first thing a person asks you when they meet you?

For me its either of the two..AHAHA you're definatelly high on drugs. Or Why so mellow? aaah withdrawal symptoms aight? always wanted to know whom you are, but you seem to be having many faces. who are you actually? I dont even know what I am anymore, to justify what I'm not. .

Hey? In a world where everyone romanticizes sad like its the new black Being able to feel mania must feel like bliss right? Right and wrong..because it all comes with a price to pay.

half the time we cant even control the things we end up doing which indirectly does damage to not just our goals and objectives but also leaves lifelong scars on our friendships and relationships and somehow these seasons never tend to cease you know .

"And before you know it winter is coming... We try so hard to get a grip on our emotions, but the tighter we grip the faster it slips, Quite so much like when we try to grip snow, but it only ends up leaving us feeling so much more numb, than we felt trying to hold it. were just tryna gain momentum in life and it all comes crashing down fluctuating, oscillating, rebounding like whatever we had before didn’t mean a thing"

Reem.

were just trying to stay away from everyone as much as we can.. We know they wouldn’t understand even if they did. We just don’t want anyone to know We don’t want to hand them out the most unstable part of our lives, Just to know later on they are using it to manipulate us

Hey? You’d ask us.. do You ever feel normal though! Yes We do...probably an hr or two. We try our best to pretend to be a normie at places where out behaviour is constantly eyed on Because apparently our behaviour is not accepted and its always just all in our heads according to so many else What’s worse is how We try so hard, to constantly battle these emotions without letting anyone know anything of it, every single day.

" Our voices had been snatched away from us somewhere amidst the cold winter nights, Where we couldn’t speak up for ourselves, Because We were too drained to do so, And then we shut out from this world, The raging storms in our minds had no escape, We constantly buried it deep inside our souls, Until it reached our threshold, And we couldn’t bear anymore. Thats when you should ever be scared of us... And you wouldn’t be surprised if We told you, Having been constantly judged upon, Amidst these rollercoaster of emotions, And tryna keep our shit together, Amidst this era where we have literally, No one to rely on, Is what brings us here to write anyways, Because we know for a certain fact, a friend has the power to deceive you, These inks do not.. "
