A bipolar in love.

By: Reem Shaikh poet

people often misintrepret our love, because were not really good at expressing the intensity of emotions we feel for someone, and we might as use notions completely opposite to love to express it, even when we never mean to, like anger; its only a result of compressed emotions we fail to truly convey, and since were always in different modes, people misintrepret our mood to relate with how we feel around them, which might not always be the case. Were just trying to not jump into love or get attached and when we do, we choose to cut off with the person entirely to prevent further damage. and so we've always ever pushed away the people we've truly loved.
