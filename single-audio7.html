The death wish.

We all know, we are all coming so close towards an inevitable doom. And if you’d ask me: Reem, how’d you want to die? I’d answer not like everyone else. I don't want to die before actually dying itself! .

I don't want to lose my breath again, I don't want to let die, what lies inside of me, while I’m still breathing, While I’m still alive, And I am not scared of witnessing, an almost eternal sleep.

For I know my soul had and will always remain immortal! Like the melody that's quite stuck in your head even when the song is quite very much over, It’ll live in people's life’s as if I were there right next to them, die in a place when no one quite realises or discovers I'm quite lifeless,

In a place where I'm lost and never found again, quite like how I lived this entire life. I know alot of you would want to be buried in funerals topped with the flowers of your choice under your picture perfect tombs, Which closes at twilight and opens at dawn. But me? I dread that shit.. But even if I do die in front of people, Promise me.you will not call anyone to my funeral..

I don’t want to internally witness anyone see me lying, as if there is no more of me.. Maybe the best way to witness a death, So I wouldn't have to face people for one last time, would be to drown it away in my personification.. wherein the deepest part of my soul lies; The oceans..

I want to die in its rippling waves, which quite resembles my untamed hair, My untamed self, I want to die in the depths of what, they cannot yet fathom. And get lost in it's serene brutality. They say it resembles, quite the depth of my eyes, depth of my soul.

And so when I die, I know, I would be, at such peace and solace knowing, Atleast I’ve died, where I know I belong, Even though I’ve lived all my life, living where I don't.

I am not suicidal, neither do I wish to die, But when I’m having anxiety attacks, it pretty much feels as if, Soon, I will be taken away and so I prepare myself for death every month, make last moment ammends before leaving, and at that moment I'm completely ready to be taken away, only it never happens. only after I'm back to my senses again, I wish to live on, And I thank all heaven’s I have a month more to live.
