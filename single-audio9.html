Love of a bipolar.

Can’t believe I've seen our constellations meet, when all it were was a bunch of disconnected stars from parallel galaxies, which were never meant to be pieced together, only to be given an illusion that maybe it were, we were meant to be.. bullshit. I wont ever come back, because even if I went back to you, I wouldn’t belong there anymore..

Yes, a bipolar loves completely, truly until you become their reason to live on, They give in soo much, and never expect an ounce in return that’s when you should know, the depths of their affection for you.

They feel soo much and express so little, even though to the world it might seem like they express a lot, The truth is, that’s not even an iota of their compressed emotions,

Some of us are verbal, some expressive, but me? Neither. Aah life can be a mumble jumble sometimes, when the only place you can truly be yourself is through your writings, for they hold a part of me I fail to say, A piece of me embodied into these combination of words, which shall live eternally on my word documents. :)

And we only try to run away from love, For our love is clearly too profound For any stardust to quite interpret. And were still scared of ever being broke again, And we try to run away from this love, over and over again only to realize further a due That this entire world has conspired to make our hearts meet over and over, no matter how much we tried to resist.

But if you know the intensity, With which WE love, My love, you’d know. How were still undoubtedly ready to fight, This invisible red string of fate, That holds our souls taut, And I know for a certain fact, that no matter the damn time, place or circumstance, We are meant to be..

For we are the embers of the same fire, The dust of the same star, Our souls had been paired before life itself, As our love echoed between the space between us.

And if you’re wondering, whether I found love, truth is I did, but only in all the wrong places, but at that instant it felt so right, that the hopeless rom in me, blindly believed, this is it, I found the one, but who knew its just pain disguised as love?
