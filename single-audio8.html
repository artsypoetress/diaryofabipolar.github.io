DEEPER PURPOSE.

I would be better off, if I had mind in the place of a heart, which I believe is more of a curse than a blessing. Especially since were just old schools living In a world where everyone craves materialism. .

And on some day’s I feel, If I could just donate my heart away to someone who needs love and a heartbeat, just seeing them live and not just exist, fills a part of my soul with life, I wouldn’t lie. I wish I could just give away all the blood that’s flowing in my veins, To someone who’s not having enough.

I wish I could give away, the glow that I’m blessed with, Just to Take out all those who are still in darkness.

I wish I could give away, all the possessions that I have today, all my little outbreaks of hype and happiness , just to give someone eternal fulfilment.

And so die in peace, after handing out each part of me, I can swear to you, I would be in a better place. I guess that’s how I choose to die, by giving my life away to someone else.

I don’t necessarily want the riches of this world, just enough to call my own, and enough to give away a part of what I own, just enough to claim, just enough to reclaim their trust in humanity again.

And they ask me: how do you give so much, yet your so full, I smile and reply: how can you pour from a half empty cup? That’s how we channel our highs to transfer happiness, and channel our lows to biuld on recovering while distancing from this world.

Everything is a blessing in disguise, if we treat it as such, a loss is never a loss, when what’s waiting for you is worth so much more than what’s gone, God never gives more pain than we can endure and He never takes away something or someone from us, without providing something worth much more than what he’s taken away,

and if you ever feel empty from giving away too much, its again, God who fills you, what’s given is never lost, it only makes it way back to us in ways we could’ve never imagined, from places we least expect it from.
