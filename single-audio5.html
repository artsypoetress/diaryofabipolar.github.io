We push everyone away, we leave.

We all are stories in some people's lives, wrong to some, even though we were right all along, a saviour to couple, and a tragedy to the countless questions that needs closure

you remind me of how my unfed heart devours to live, as the poetry that I cannot rephrase into words, your like that manuscript that is as fluent in silence as my heart has finally learnt to embrace this feeling of wanting to let go and not hold on at the same time..

so before I leave, let me tell you because you, you were the colour that this colourless haze of mind of mine longed for , and right now, even though this love has devastated me, even though you were never mine, or were almost, I don’t even know what we were,

so before I leave, let me tell you, yes, I’m leaving you, even though I am undoubtedly in love with every piece of you, I’m leaving, even though every part of me want to stay. I’m leaving because, I’m unstable and I don’t want to pull you into hell with me.

I’m leaving because I love you more than you’ve ever loved me, I’m leaving because I’m damaged, and you deserved to be healed, I’m leaving because I have no reason to hold onto, what we have anymore, I’m leaving, because internally I’m in a very bad place and You deserve the best of all worlds,

Even though, your efforts to keep me, explained every thing. I’m leaving because, I’m scared to get attached to you, To love somebody, who doesn’t and has never loved me, I’m leaving, because I have no reason to hold on, And that’s a good enough reason to leave.

But before I leave, let me tell you, the darkest part of you reminded me of the most powerful scream that my soul had ever encountered , not wanting to see you in the same doom I've seen myself, I lost myself, trying to save you,

even when I had absolute no reason to have to, even if that meant I had to die. the emotional death, where I'm not even a part of this world anymore, where I'm living on the pages of some another dimension.

with no one to hold me just close enough, just a few you'll be okay and your the strongest women I’ve ever known of, no one to actually hold this bruised heart of mine and tell me, your pain is not yours, its ours. We go through shit together, remember?

silly of me to think their were more lovers like me someplace in the corners of this cruel cruel world I tried saving you, trust me i did because if I died, and you survived I would've considered myself to have lived a good life and that's the love, I had in for you,

this is not a final note, to make you realize.. two wrongs do not make one right this is a final bid, a forever goodbye

and that’s how I’ve left everyone I’VE EVER LOVED, before I could even call them my own. Only because they failed to truly understand me, they failed to hold on when I was at my weakest, when I needed them the most.
