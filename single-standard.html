Oh you want to know me, where should we start?.

Should I say whom I am? Or whom I'm not? Or should I say what I should be? Or should I tell what still remains, the very ruins, is it sometimes that the pain that makes it surface level and what finally makes you dwell into the maybe’s into the what not’s, into the what if’s? .

Trust me, I try, These walls higher than the “what if nots, the what if knots”? Do you really listen to me, or do you just hear the vibrations of my sound making its way into your life like a wave , gently and consuming you completely before you know it. but let me ask you, is she really Dark, damaged, toxic? Or just broke into a million pieces that can only phase together, by her healing love, she’s very much deprived of.

Read me, through my pauses, not solely through my words, decipher me through the unobvious, what can't be seen, but only felt. But here I warn you, don’t risk to unravel me, if your scared of heights, if your scared of fall. If your scared to reach to my highs and jump to your lows, I'm damaged, don’t play gamble with your life, falling for me would be equivalent to loosing yourself and never finding again of what you lost, and never finding again of what so left, over pieces you’ve ever found.

Reem.

Im damaged, I hurt, but never mean to, I inflict pain, but never intend to. We only give what we are or what we have become. Trying to understand me, I dare you not, if your scared of drowning, for I promise you, there’s no way out once you jump into my depths,

"Decode me through the context, not merely through my code, I guess there’s just a fault in my code. That I cant debug and you cant spot even after being given the cues"

You don’t know me, all you know is im danger in a beautiful façade, damage to a point I call it my only escape, I bury it deep within, but somehow it never stays there Who am i? I wish I knew. What am I not? you wish you did.
