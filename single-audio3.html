How does a bipolar feel?

We feel so distant like were seeing this world through a mist, like our gaze is loosing focus, fogged up vision, everything looks like a blurr, sounds like one, we want things to clear up before it consumes us once again, reckon the forces before the forces reckon with us,

does this happen to you? You shut off from life to a point, you can’t even hear when someone screams your name, and when you do, you feel like their calling out for you, from very far away? Your so distant from reality, your mind never in the same place, your body is. Its like your sitting at your grave waiting to be taken away.

Externally All you hear is indistinct chattering, Internally all you hear are inner voices, which are not even your own, or have they become? You never know. does this ever happen to you? Where you’re not living or dead, just existing on survival mode. When you see your reflection and you don’t recognize who she is?

For she’s an entirely different person, every alternate interval, She’s hard to understand, hardest to keep, the only terms she’s ever been accused of, are either of the two, toxic positivity and toxic negativity, depicting her abnormal highs and lows, My reaction to things have become so inconsistent,

Like I’m different people, reacting at different times, living different lives. I don’t want to believe I’m not normal, neither do I want to take pills. I am so paranoid, that I cant even trust counsellors, therapists or psychiatrists at this point. That’s why sometimes I black out, because theirs not a soul I can rely on, And so I don’t get attached, I don’t do commitments and stay away from people, Because they only do more damage, than ever heal.

I don’t know where and how and what I deserved to be on the receiving end of many mixed emotions that I cant phase into words. Atleast it drives me to creative or extremely reckoning outcomes, I’m grateful for the first. We have not chosen to live with this, wanting to know whom we really are, amidst multiple personas and being deceptive to our heart’s decision for us, we loose ourselves every single time.

I don’t know whom I am, for the fifth time in this entire week, the moment I feel I’m this close to healing, and just the next moment I’ve completely lost myself back to phase 1. I don’t want to seek help, I just ever want to be understood. I always reckon my relations with people, it never lasts, I don’t even know at this point whether I withdraw to save them or save myself. Decisions so impulsive I would decide to leave you forever in a minute and never come back, I’m so dangerous, that it scares me, I only ever understand everyone, but no one ever understands me. And it scares me. To be so much, to give so much, and get back of such little significance.
