Mania & Depressive episodes

but what does It feel like to live my life? They ask.

Imagine, waking up everyday feeling like your hallucinating and not even a part of this world, claustrophobic yet trapped in this space, I can’t even call my own. Myself.

Laughing for no apparent reason until your insides hurt and switching to devouring in pain over again, you wish you can control what’s happening, but even you don’t have any answers for this, you want to know, but they don’t let.

That’s how it feels. A bad place, my mind, but all you see is the light, that’s all she ever lets you see, she can’t let two people in, in her reckoning well, can she?

you only ever know what she ever lets you, you only never know what she doesn’t,

What makes you happy, only brings you pain, what brings you pain, only make you feel alive again. What else do your expect when All life you were forced to be something your not?

Until that’s what you became, until that’s all that remains within you, agony, bearing stories I would’ve double checked would reach no soul,

I write, only to burn, and as it burns it feels like my pain is burning away, and just when I feel like I'm getting better, I'm only getting worse.

I was brought up to, to hate what I am And try embrace what I’m not, everyone is right and I’m wrong,

To love what I don’t, to pretend to not love, whom I do. And so I started hating the things I absolutely love, loving the things, that inflict me pain,

I still feel unreal like I’m high on something, only I know I’m not. as I type this, I lowkey feel like I’m somewhere else, neither living nor dead

Devoured in Pain till it became a permanent escape, an adrenaline rush that’s all that remains, a purpose to live on, that’s pain to me.

And so I devour in it, and let my soul sink in completely, what was never mine or will ever be

phases stuck in faces, faces stuck in phases, changes with circumstances, nothing constant, its only a phase it will fade, its only a face, it will change, but who are you? Whom am I?

you seen this part of me, you seen that part of me, you seen soo many parts. that you don’t even know, which one is me anymore, are you alive? Or just existing?

I live as a parasite on these pages as I type, isn’t that enough?
