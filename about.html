Meet my split Ruby.

We are mismatches between our physical reality and your perceptions, a countless people are in a delusion of what we really are, for they have countless perceptions of us and when it fails to meet the version they know, everyone suddenly becomes sceptic, that are we even real? Or just optical illusions? If only they knew.

Ruby

Meet my split Ruby, a true introvert, she lives as my shadow, yet she repeatedly tries to convey how she didn’t choose the darkness, rather the darkness chose her, not many have witnessed her personally, for she prefers to hide away from this world, rather. because those who have, never accepted her; its like she’s her evil counterpart. She’s the artist in her, the writer, the critic, the split who believes love is a myth, pessimistic, sadist, phychopathic, nihilist.


Reem, the hyper active split, something over the board social, she literally transfers life into people, well that’s what many phrase her as, impulsive hella, people could swear she got balls of steel, She’s the confidence in her, the split who fights and conquers, the lover, the warrior. the healer, the counsellor, the therapist, and a deep believer in the power of timing, fate and love.

some days she can throw laughing fits and on the others she unknowingly plots her own reckoning, when she’s Reem she varies from positive to toxic positive, when she’s Ruby she’s nothing but a pessimist. Some call her a pessimistic optimist, when she tries to be normal, to not stand out, where she knows she wouldn’t be accepted, and that’s why she’s almost never at home, and that’s why she’s always tried to find a home someplace else, sometimes in somebody else, a best friend/ a potential love interest.

but trying to be something she’s not, leaves her drained to a point, she needs to cut out from life and people for days at a stretch that’s when Ruby is activated. mostly everyone have seen her, not many have known, so when and if they witness a part of her split “Ruby”, the only question at the back of their heads will and still remains, which part of her? Is truly her.

I don’t complain, but I’m still trying to embrace, to be able to be two people, in one person.. many times, that’s how I get away with things, but it still leaves me eternally unstable, because I’ve hid Ruby from this entire world, and she needs an escape, and so she comes here to express herself. she just trying to accept herself, which she finds through venting her soul into words, she wants to heal not just through, being there for herself, but by making the world realize there are countless like her. And we all can heal through writing, only if we try,

She, like a 45Million people worldwide, who are diagnosed with bipolar tendencies, just needs to be understood and needs the world to understand that a person can be both light and darkness, pure and impure. Halo and horns. Just because you’ve witnessed the yin, doesn’t mean that the yan is non existent. Just because when they met you their light overpowers their darkness, doesn’t mean that, that’s really the case.

The author is not having split personality disorder, these characters are solely two versions that live on two opposite poles of life, to depict the extremes they live on.

based on a real life story
