Seeking to be found, as lost as ever.

Loose yourself in the wild, and the uncertainties will make you find yourself again. lost in these trails seeking to be found, You tell me everything has its time, then why is the waiting not having an end point I'm left in delusions, delusions left in time, hoping to believe in eternity, Hoping to believe patience is virtue, and good things take time, But bad things take longer, or happen almost instantly, So when it finally happens how can I be sure whether its good or bad, You tell me, be you, when I’m more me then everyone else,

Then tell me how for the sake of sanity or insanity? Should you expect of me to sink into whom I truly am, The me who’s been lost enough, and not yet been found.

Where the Atlantic and the pacific meet, That’s exactly how it feels to be me, to be the meeting point of insanity of two different intensities

These densities that only consume me, until the raging storms ever seem to ever ask me, Reem, How many times did u loose yourself before you found yourself ever again? Until my heart became one with my head Until Skeptic. Paranoid that’s all I remain

How do you become one? With something you can’t even call your own? The same way you become one, with someone that’s not yours,

Like I became one with Ruby, my dark side, who almost never warns me before creeping in, Like I became one with all the people I’ve ever loved and never loved me back, Like I became one with my demons, with my angels alike,

Why are you in love with a memory that goes distant every time you blink your eyes? I’m only trying to accept what I feel, How long will you keep accepting something that’s not yours? Or will ever be?

If only I could love the way, I’ve loved everyone else If only I could guide me like I guide them, If only I could accept myself as my eternal permanent homage, And not seek for it, from anyone else,

If only I could paint words and write pictures, Like the word that paints a thousand pictures, And the picture that paints a million words,

So I could write my way out of the haze And paint my destination, of what lies, beyond my gaze today,

If only I could stop Running away from the depths of my conscience When I know how deep of what I need right now, is closure.

The closure that lives in the moments I left behind, The moments that are yet to be found again, Somewhere where my horizon lies, and all that’s left is crimson and my survival guide.
