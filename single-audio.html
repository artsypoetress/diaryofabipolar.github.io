Describing Bipolar tendancies.

Oh you want to know me, where should we start? Should I say whom I am? Or whom I’m not? Or should I say what I should be? Or should I tell what still remains like the very ruins that are lost deep within me,

is it sometimes that the pain that makes it to the surface level of your entire existence and only then it finally makes you dwell, into the maybe’s, the what not’s, into the what if’s?

is it really pain that fades away with time? Or is it something else that stays afloat and then sinks within. Do you ever listen to the oceans? Do you ever really listen to me, or do you just hear the vibrations making its way into your life like a wave with a decievingly calm smile , and in a blink of an eye, your drowning in my depths, loosing conscience as your lungs inflate with my reckoning, and completely consumes as you exhale

Until all your left in is Darkness, you hit rock bottom and finally see what I am deep beneath, cold, beautiful but cold, resilient, for I know whom to save, and whom to drown. I bury my agony deep within, until it needs an escape,on some days I’m a tornado, on the others I’m sound you meditate to, the answer to your prayers, you would never know which wave would calm you and which would drown, for that’s my impulse.

You see everyone painting the sea shore, the sunshine, the stills seas, you don’t see many trying to jump into trying to figure out what really lies within, maybe, that’s why not many get us.

From a calm distance, I love admiring her from the shore of my soul. To jump into her into her soul is just the easy part, to calm her chaos in a way that wont make her loose herself over again, now that’s the real struggle.

Do you still want to know who I am? should I say? Or should I rather say how I want to be interpreted, because no one ever seems to get it right, not on the first try, not at their last.

Read me, through my pauses, not solely through my words, do you care when I don’t say a thing? For I’m bottling these emotions to explode in some way, I'm dangerous, I'm not just the oceans but the volcanoes hiding deep within hoping not to erupt , destructive, I'm explosive.

decipher me through the unobvious, what cant be seen, but only felt. Do you only see my softness, do you not see me through my blues, the storms that took everything away until all I was left was oblivion, I'm down yet so resilent.

My bad, I almost forgot you only ever view me through those rose colored lenses, and fail to hold onto me, once I grow out my thorns, hey I don’t blame you.

Its not you, its me. I’m damaged. And all my life I’ve been seeking for someone wise enough to mend me; but I haven’t yet found someone who came with the instruction manual of “how to fix my broken pieces”

So atleast for the sake of peace, serenity. I could not destroy the life within and outside me for once, I only always intended to save, and that explains of how 99% of the life in the oceans is still left to be discovered.

When would it be? I wish I knew. I only want to save, but no one understands me enough to save me. They are scared to reach to my highs and jump to their lows, but the entire world is just scared of heights and falls

I’m damaged, I hurt, but never mean to, I inflict pain, but never intend to. We only give what we are or what we have become.

I only push everyone away because I know no one would stay, I can smell intentions from miles away

there’s no way out once you jump into my depths, unless your willing to stay, there’s no other way, the souls who tame the ocean, if you exist. Where are you? If you’re alive? Where are you hiding?

You don’t know me, all you know is I’m danger in a beautiful façade, damaged to a point I call it my only escape, I bury it deep within, but somehow it never stays there.

I am both the storms and the calm that follows it, I am bipolar; but I do not let it define me, I am resilient, I’ve battled the storms and sailed through my rage,

People misinterpret, I’ve made evens with it, you want to know me, so do I wish you did. But I believe its more important for you to know rather than what I am. What I am not.
