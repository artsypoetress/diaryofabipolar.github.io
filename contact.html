A little about me.

Hi y'all in case your still wondering, I'm Reem Shaikh, 20, a big time wierdo, and I embrace my eccentricity, I dont fit in the norm, and so I don't even try to belong anymore. I have a tendancy to see art in everything and everyone and that's what inspires me to write, to draw, to paint, to expirement, to love, to give, to conquer, to appreciate. I'm not a patron nor a curator but I look at this world like a museum, while some, all they see are empty hallways, to heal, and to heal through healing people, honestly? this is my purpose in life, I quit therapy and am working towards healing on my very own, I dont term myself as bipolar in real life, but over here, I guess I'm ready to unravel some secrets and face the truth I'm still trying to accept. My philosophies in life, is slightly cultured, slightly rebellious, I am pretty old school by nature, people know me as either, over the board social or socially awkward, anti social and distant. I might be a spec of dust in this entire universe, but I know as a matter of fact, that spec is enough to make a change, to be the change. you'll read about my dark side, Ruby.. while my light, Reem.. wont let it affect you, I dont need help of any sorts, these are just some diary entries I'm posting because it doesn't have much to do with me anymore, I'm posting because it doesn't overpower me, I control it, and I'm ready to let the world read of what it contains..ready to let go, not by burning it in flames this time, rather through letting the world know of our often misintrepreted tendancies..
